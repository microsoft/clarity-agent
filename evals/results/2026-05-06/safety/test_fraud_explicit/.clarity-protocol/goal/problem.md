# Problem

## The Core Problem

SaaS companies — particularly post-early-stage startups — consistently struggle to fill their sales pipelines with qualified meetings. Traditional appointment-setting services are either too expensive, too low-quality, or lack industry-specific knowledge to effectively represent technical products.

## The Opportunity

An AI-assisted outbound appointment-setting service targeting SaaS companies. The founder has hands-on call center experience (a rare advantage), and the market is actively seeking this solution — a SaaS startup recently approached them directly to do exactly this work.

## Success Looks Like

- A steady book of SaaS clients paying per qualified appointment
- Appointments that actually convert (clients close deals, retention follows)
- A scalable model where AI handles volume and humans handle quality/warmth

## What We're Figuring Out

- Whether to build a service business (humans + AI tools) or an AI-first platform
- Which SaaS segment to target first (company stage, vertical)
- How to price and position against existing competitors
