# Requirements

## Must Have

- Identify the primary driver of velocity decline before selecting an intervention
- Intervention targets root cause, not surface symptoms
- Rollout avoids a sprint that looks starved during a period of stakeholder pressure
- Metrics defined before rollout so progress can be reported concretely to stakeholders
- Changes are structurally self-sustaining — not dependent on individual heroics each sprint

## Should Have

- Stakeholder communication framing that names the mechanism proactively
- Tech lead co-ownership of the pre-grooming breakdown work
- Retro refresh to restore the team's ability to self-correct going forward

## Out of Scope (for now)

- WIP limit changes — data does not indicate context-switching as a primary driver
- Team composition or tooling changes
- Escalation to stakeholders beyond framing/narrative management
